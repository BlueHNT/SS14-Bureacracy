[head=2]Contraband Turnover Program[/head]

[bold]Program Overview:[/bold]
The Contraband Turnover Program allows for the exchange of FUCs (Frontier Uplink Coins) for contraband items in compliance with current regulations. For every FUC submitted, a payout of [color=#ff0000]2,000 spesos[/color] is granted. This program is intended for civilian or station personnel who possess contraband but wish to comply with the amnesty efforts.

[bold]Important Notes:[/bold]

This program does not apply to UIV (Unidentified Vessel) contraband. The NFSD maintains exclusive authority over the handling of such contraband.
Contraband turned in will be assessed, and the respective amount of FUCs will be credited to the holder.
